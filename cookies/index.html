Cookie Policy

What cookies and local storage this site uses, why, and how to turn them off.

What a cookie is (30-second version)

A cookie is a tiny text file that the browser stores on your device so a site can recognise you on the next page view. Some cookies are essential (session management); others track analytics or marketing attribution. This site uses a narrow set described below.

Cookies this site sets

Name Set by Purpose Lifetime
bh_cookie_consent First-party Remembers your Accept / Reject choice on the consent banner. 365 days
_ga, _ga_* Google Analytics 4 Distinguishes unique visitors. Aggregate traffic + event analytics. 2 years
_gid Google Analytics 4 Distinguishes unique visitors within a 24-hour window. 24 hours
_clck, _clsk, CLID Microsoft Clarity Session replay + heatmap analytics (text and form fields masked). 1 year

Consent Mode v2

Categories used by this site: analytics_storage, ad_storage, ad_user_data, ad_personalization, functionality_storage, personalization_storage, security_storage. Default state is granted (opt-out model). The consent banner flips every category except security_storage to denied if you click "Reject".

How to turn tracking off

  1. Click Reject on the consent banner if it appears. If it's already dismissed, clear bh_cookie_consent from your browser cookies and reload — the banner will re-appear.
  2. Or use your browser's "Do Not Track" / cookie-blocking settings.
  3. Or install a content-blocker like uBlock Origin or Privacy Badger — this site does not try to detect or circumvent them.
